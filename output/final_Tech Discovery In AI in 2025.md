**H2 - What is Notion AI?**

Notion AI refers to the use of artificial intelligence in the Notion workspace. Notion is an all-in-one productivity platform that allows users to create, organize, and manage their digital notes, tasks, and projects with ease. By leveraging AI, Notion can automatically suggest connections between ideas, provide personalized recommendations, and even generate custom templates.

AI-powered chatbots are being integrated into Notion to help users with complex queries, providing instant answers and freeing up time for more important tasks.
