The Future of Manufacturing is Here

Get ready to revolutionize how BEST Group operates! Cognite Data Fusion is about to transform our raw data into powerful insights.

Introducing Cognite Data Fusion

Imagine a world where all of BEST Group's manufacturing data works together seamlessly, providing smart insights to optimize our operations. That's the power of Cognite Data Fusion (CDF). CDF creates a central hub for all our data, turning it into a "digital twin" of our operations. This isn't just about collecting data; it's about using that data to make smarter decisions and drive innovation.

What's New

Cognite is always improving, and their latest achievement proves their dedication to being the best:

  • DNV Compliance for Digital Twins (2023-03): Cognite Data Fusion® is the first in the industry to be DNV-certified for digital twins, meeting DNV-RP-A204 standards. This certification confirms that CDF is reliable and trustworthy for building digital twins, especially in the energy sector. Learn more here.

Why This Matters

In today's world, data is incredibly valuable. But raw data alone isn't enough. Cognite Data Fusion helps us make sense of our data by connecting the dots and giving us a complete picture of BEST Group's operations. This means we can make better decisions, improve our processes, and boost the performance of our equipment. The result? We become more efficient, save money, become more sustainable and gain a significant advantage over the competition. CDF is designed to grow with us and work with new technologies, making it a smart investment for the future.

How BEST Group Could Use It

Cognite Data Fusion opens up many exciting possibilities for improving BEST Group's manufacturing:

  • Predictive Maintenance: Imagine knowing when a piece of equipment is likely to fail *before* it actually happens. By analyzing data from sensors, we can schedule maintenance proactively, reducing downtime and extending the life of our equipment.
  • Process Optimization: Create a digital copy of our entire manufacturing process to identify areas for improvement, streamline workflows, and achieve new levels of efficiency.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Get a real-time overview of all manufacturing operations on a single dashboard. This allows operators to quickly spot and fix problems, ensuring smooth and continuous production.
  • Supply Chain Optimization: Combine data from suppliers, logistics, and our internal systems to create a transparent and responsive supply chain, shortening lead times and improving our ability to adapt to change.
  • Quality Control: Use data analysis to identify defects early on, preventing faulty products from reaching customers and protecting our reputation.
